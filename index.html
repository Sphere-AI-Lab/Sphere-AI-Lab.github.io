SphereLab.

Scalable Principles for Learning and Reasoning Lab @ CUHK

We believe the next leap in AI won't come from blindly stacking more parameters — it will come from finding the right principles. The simple ones. The ones that truly scale. So we hunt for them.

We work on the principles that shape how machines learn — the structures, symmetries, and quiet assumptions that decide what a model can generalize to. We believe the right idea is often worth more than another order of magnitude of compute, and we work to turn the ones we find into functional algorithms and systems.

How we work.  Curiosity over trends. Depth over speed. Insight before application.
