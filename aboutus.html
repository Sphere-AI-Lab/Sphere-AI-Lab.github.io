About Us

SphereLab (Scalable Principles for Learning and Reasoning Lab) advances the foundations and practice of machine learning and AI reasoning. We explore scalable learning principles, formal reasoning, and their synergy, building systems that are data-efficient, reliable, and interpretable. Our interests span large-scale representation learning, language models, optimization, formal methods, and applications across science and engineering.
